Heimlich on a Baby

  1. Put your baby facedown on your arm (support their head)
  2. Give 5 back slaps with heel of palm
  3. Turn the baby upward and on your thigh
  4. Put 2 fingers below the chest and push inward and upward

Repeat steps until the baby stops choking or 911 arrives
